## Usage rules
- Blue is the primary action color.
- Red is the accent color.
- Light backgrounds and white cards dominate the UI.
- Newsletter outputs prioritize school branding over SchoolAmplified branding.

## School-level overrides
Allow schools to set:
- logo
- primary color
- secondary color
- accent color
- footer/contact/social details
- preferred template
